Measurement Framework

Metrics Hierarchy

Level 1 - North Star: Superfans (the one metric that matters most)

Level 2 - Primary KPIs (4): Revenue, Brand Awareness, CAC, LTV

Level 3 - Secondary KPIs (12): AOV, Repeat Rate, NPS, Conversion, etc.

Level 4 - Operational (30+): Daily tracking metrics

Reporting Cadence

  • Daily: Revenue, orders, traffic (Leadership, 2-min review)
  • Weekly: All primary KPIs, trends (Full team, 30-min)
  • Monthly: Comprehensive report (Board, investors, 60-min)
  • Quarterly: Strategic review + roadmap progress (Board, 90-min)
