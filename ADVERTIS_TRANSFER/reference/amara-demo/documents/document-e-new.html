2. Big Idea - Creative Concept

"Every Thread Tells a Story"

From the hands of artisans in Lagos to the streets of New York, each garment carries generations of craft, culture, and pride. When you wear AMARA, you don't just wear fashion‚Äîyou wear history, you wear artistry, you wear a story waiting to be told.

Narrative Foundation

The campaign unveils the journey of a single garment‚Äîfrom the cotton fields of Burkina Faso, to the weaving workshops of Kente cloth artisans, to the dye masters using ancient indigo techniques, finally arriving in the wardrobe of a modern professional.

We follow real artisan Aisha, 52, whose grandmother taught her weaving patterns passed down for 7 generations. Her hands create the fabric that becomes AMARA's signature pieces. This is her thread. This is her story.

Campaign Essence

Emotional Core: Pride in heritage, connection to craft, meaningful consumption

Functional Promise: Premium fashion that honors tradition while embracing modern design

Call to Action: "What's Your Story?" - Invitation to join the AMARA movement, share your heritage, celebrate your narrative

3. Axe Cr√©atif - Creative Direction

Visual Direction
  • Photography Style: Documentary-inspired, authentic, warm natural lighting
  • Color Palette: Earthy terracotta, deep forest greens, warm golds, natural textures

Execution Principles
  1. Authenticity First: Real artisans, real stories, real names - no stock photography, no actors
  2. Cultural Respect: Collaborate with cultural consultants, compensate featured artisans fairly, accurate representation
  3. Sensory Rich: Show texture, show process, show hands creating - make craft tangible
  4. Emotional Resonance: Connect heritage to modern identity - bridge tradition and contemporary life
  5. Inclusive Invitation: "Your story matters too" - not just African heritage, everyone has a story

4. Pistes Cr√©atives - Creative Directions

Three creative execution routes, each exploring different facets of the "Threads of Heritage" concept.

Piste 1: "Master Class" Video Series

Concept: 6-episode documentary series profiling artisans behind AMARA pieces. Each 3-5 minute episode follows one master crafts person.

Execution: Cinematic quality, subtitled interviews, close-up craftsmanship shots, before/after garment journey

Distribution: YouTube hero content, Instagram IGTV, website feature, PR hook for media coverage

Budget Allocation: $80k (production) + $40k (paid amplification)

Piste 2: "Wear Your Story" UGC Campaign

Concept: Invite customers to share their heritage stories via #WearYourStory. Curate best stories, feature on website/social.

Execution: Contest mechanic (prize: free garment + profile feature), story submission portal, community voting

Distribution: Instagram/TikTok native, influencer seeding, PR amplification

Budget Allocation: $30k (prizes + platform) + $50k (influencer seeding)

Piste 3: "Heritage Tour" Pop-Up Experiences

Concept: 3 city pop-up installations (NYC, London, Lagos) - immersive storytelling + shopping experience

Execution: Physical space designed like artisan workshop, live weaving demonstrations, meet-the-maker events

Distribution: Ticketed events (free), press preview, Instagram-worthy photo moments

Budget Allocation: $150k (venue + production) + $30k (PR/promo)

Recommended Execution Mix

Phase 1 (M4-M6): Launch with Piste 1 (Video Series) as hero content + Piste 2 (UGC Campaign) for engagement

Phase 2 (M7-M9): Add Piste 3 (Pop-Ups) in key markets for experiential layer + continued video/UGC

Rationale: Build awareness with rich video content, activate community with UGC, deepen connection with physical experiences.

7. Superfans Program

Community Cultivation & Advocacy

The Superfans Program is AMARA's North Star Metric focus: building a community of 5,000 deeply engaged brand advocates who don't just buy‚Äîthey believe, belong, and amplify. These are customers who:

  • Purchase 3+ times per year (high repeat rate)
  • Share content organically (UGC creators)
  • Refer new customers (NPS 9-10 promoters)
  • Attend events and engage with community
  • Co-create with brand (feedback, product ideas)

Superfan Cultivation Tactics

Tier 1: Recognition

Exclusive badge, early access to collections, birthday surprises, personalized thank-you notes from founders.

Tier 2: Access

Private community Slack, quarterly virtual meet-ups with artisans, behind-the-scenes content, product development input.

Tier 3: Co-Creation

Advisory council (20 superfans), collaborative capsule collections, ambassador program, revenue share on referrals.
