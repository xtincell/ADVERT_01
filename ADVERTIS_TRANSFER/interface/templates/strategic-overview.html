Risk (Doc R) - Top strategic risks and mitigation strategies

Risk 1: Supply Chain Sustainability

Score: 16 (High)

Description: Artisan partners fail to meet demand, quality standards slip, or labor practices exposed as unfair.

Probability: 4 (Likely) | Impact: 4 (Major)
Affects: Brand reputation, product availability, values credibility

Mitigation:

  • Diversify artisan partnerships (10+ partners, no >20% dependency)
  • Fair Trade certification + regular audits
  • Transparent supply chain tracking (blockchain)
  • Capacity planning with 6-month lead time

Risk 2: Cultural Appropriation Accusation

Score: 12 (High)

Description: Brand accused of appropriating African culture without proper attribution, compensation, or understanding.

Probability: 3 (Possible) | Impact: 4 (Major)
Affects: Brand reputation, customer trust, media perception

Mitigation:

  • Cultural consultants review all creative work
  • Artisans prominently credited (names, stories, photos)
  • Fair revenue sharing with artisan communities
  • Transparent about founder background + intentions

Risk 3: Premium Pricing Resistance

Score: 12 (High)

Description: Target customers unwilling to pay premium prices ($420-$1,200), expect fast-fashion pricing.

Probability: 4 (Likely) | Impact: 3 (Moderate)
Affects: Sales velocity, customer acquisition, revenue targets

Mitigation:

  • Education-focused marketing (cost of craftsmanship)
  • Transparent pricing breakdown (artisan wages, materials)
  • Lifetime value positioning (cost-per-wear over 10+ years)
  • Entry-price products ($420) to lower barrier

Risk 4: Social Media Crisis

Score: 9 (Medium)

Description: Viral negative sentiment from brand action, statement, or employee behavior. Boycott calls, media scrutiny.

Probability: 3 (Possible) | Impact: 3 (Moderate)
Affects: Brand reputation, sales, employee morale

Mitigation:

  • Values alignment review for all campaigns
  • Social listening 24/7 (Brandwatch)
  • Crisis communication playbook ready
  • Brand advocates network to amplify positive voices

Scenario Planning (3 Scenarios)

Best Case: "Heritage Boom"

Sustainable African fashion trends mainstream. Celebrity endorsements. Strong demand exceeds supply. Expand faster than planned.

Base Case: "Steady Growth"

Plan unfolds as expected. Challenges manageable. Metrics on track. Year 2 profitability achieved. Sustainable trajectory.

Worst Case: "Recession + Crisis"

Economic downturn reduces luxury spending. Supply chain crisis. Cultural appropriation scandal. Extend runway, pivot strategy.
