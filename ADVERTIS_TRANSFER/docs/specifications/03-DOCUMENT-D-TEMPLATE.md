**Template:**
```yaml

  design_principles: *
    - principle_1:
        name: "[Principle Name]"

    - principle_2:

    - principle_3: [...]
    - principle_4: [...]
    - principle_5: [...]

  visual_dna_keywords: *
    - "[Adjective 1 - e.g., 'Modern']"

    - "[Adjective 3 - e.g., 'Confident']"
```
